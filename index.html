Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your web design packages?

Our comprehensive packages include custom design, responsive development, CMS integration, SEO optimization, hosting setup, and 30 days of post-launch support.

How long does it take to build a custom website?

Most projects are completed within 2-4 weeks. Complex e-commerce sites may take 6-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, we offer monthly maintenance plans that include updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support to keep your site running smoothly.

Will my website be optimized for search engines?

Absolutely! We build all sites with SEO best practices, including optimized code, fast loading speeds, mobile responsiveness, and proper meta data structure.
